Welcome to the Runeforge Wiki!

We are a community dedicated in creating Mods for League of Legends. If you see help, you shall find it in here, with out evergrowing list of guides and informations all about modding League of Legends.

 

Beginners Guide!

If you are new to our community, we highly recommend starting here, where you find a List with nessecities and starter guides to get a leap into your new journey! Don't worry, if you still need help you can always ask in our Discord Server to help figuring things out.

 

 

 

 

 

Want to contribute?

We welcome your contributions to the Wiki! If you possess valuable knowledge not yet covered, or skills in programs relevant to our work, please don't hesitate to join our Discord server. Once there, feel free to ping a Mod/Staff Member; we're eager to assist you in sharing your expertise and helping our community grow. Your unique insights can significantly enhance our resources, benefiting all users. We look forward to collaborating with you!

 

Runeforge.io & wiki.Runeforge.io

With the development of Runeforge.dev, we've taken a significant stride towards greater independence from third-party page builders and providers that often impose limitations. This transition means Runeforge.dev will now serve as the self-developed and self-managed web platform specifically tailored for League of Legends modders.

This exciting change also includes an update to our wiki's address. The official wiki URL will change to wiki.Runeforge.dev. But don't worry, we're ensuring a seamless transition, just as we did with Runeforge.io. Both the old and new URLs will continue to direct you to the same comprehensive wiki for a limited time during this migration period, ensuring uninterrupted access to all your modding resources. This move allows us to offer a more robust and flexible environment to support the League of Legends modding community.
